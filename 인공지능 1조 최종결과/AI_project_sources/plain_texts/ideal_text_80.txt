SOAPSToneS How to Analyze a Written Work(on Plan Your Own) SPEAKER Who is the author or narrator and what are his or her characteristics? What point of view (first or third) is being used? OCCASIOM What is the time and place of the writing to happen? AUDIENCE What group of readers does the speaker hope to reach? PURPOSE What is the message? What does the speaker want the audience to think or do after reading this text? SUBJECT What is he main idea or topic? TONE What is the author's attitude towards the topic? STYLE How does the author write? How do choice of words (diction), sentence construction (syntax) or imagery (vivid description) convey the author's feelings?
